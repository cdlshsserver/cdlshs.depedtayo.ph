CRECENCIA DRUCILA LOPEZ SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Crecencia Drucila Lopez Senior High School is a Public senior high school that instills in its graduates the values of commitment, competence, compassion so that they may become persons-for-others who are builders of society. The Senior High School encompasses Grades 11 and 12 of CDLSHS Basic Education.

Our mission at CRECENCIA DRUCILA LOPEZ is to provide a supportive and challenging environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. Established in 2016, we have a proud tradition of preparing students for success in college and beyond.

This Our School Strands at
Crecencia Drucila Lopez Senior High Schools

Crecencia Drucila Lopez Senior High School offers diverse academic and tvl strands catering to students' interests and career aspirations, providing a focused curriculum for excelling in their chosen fields.

HUMMS

Humanities and Social Sciences

HUMSS explores human society, culture, and behavior, fostering critical thinkers and communicators through history, philosophy, literature, and arts, promoting understanding and shaping the world.

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering And Mathematics

STEM, or Science, Technology, and Engineering, is a strand that prepares students for success in fields such as math, science, and technology, fostering innovation and knowledge.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management

ABM prepares future entrepreneurs, managers, and accountants with financial literacy, business ethics, and management principles, preparing them to lead and innovate in the commerce and enterprise world.

TVL

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood

TVL offers practical training in various trades and skills, preparing students for the workforce or specialized training, including culinary arts, automotive repair, information technology / Programming, and welding.
